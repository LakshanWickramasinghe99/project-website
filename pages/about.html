GUIDED BY

// SUPERVISOR
Mr. Didula Chamara
Department of Information Technology
Faculty of Computing, SLIIT

This research was conducted under the supervision of Mr. Didula Chamara, whose guidance shaped the technical direction and evaluation methodology of the Criminal Identification Support System. Their expertise in computer vision and information security provided a rigorous framework for the system's design decisions.

The project benefited greatly from regular feedback sessions that pushed the team to prioritise real-world operational validity over laboratory-idealised performance — a principle reflected throughout the evaluation strategy.
